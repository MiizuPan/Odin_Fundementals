Bibimbap

Here's how to make the healthy and yummy korean dish!

You will need:

  • Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Steak
  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Gochujang

Steps to making your perfect Bibimbap

  1. Cut the cucumber into sticks and stir with a spoonful of gochujang in a bowl
  2. Cook the spinach until bright green and wilted
  3. Drain the spinach squeezing out as much moisture as possible, set aside
  4. Cook the carrots until soft, then stir in the cucumber mixture and add red pepper flakes
  5. In a different skillet, brown the beefli>
  6. Fry eggs until the yolks are runny, but whites are firm
  7. Top each serving of rice with the spinach mixture, beef and the cucumber mixture.
  8. Place an egg atop each serving, then drizzle with sesame oil, sesame seeds and gochujang(if desired)

Mix well to your liking and Enjoy!
